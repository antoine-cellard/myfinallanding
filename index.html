Who are we ?


Zumo is the result of the journey of a group of curious, nature-loving explorers and tasteful lovers. In the four corners of the world, they fully discovered all the beauty and generosity of nature... Every day, Zumo offers recipes of smoothies and freshly squeezed fruit juices. Made with 100% natural ingredients, our juices and smoothies are free of added sugar, colourless and preservative and without any artificial aroma... Zumo is a “brand brand” that unites a community of expertise (farmers, researchers, herbalists, nutritionists, sociologists, chefs). Every day Zumo explores tastes associations...
